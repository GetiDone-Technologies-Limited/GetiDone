Platform Architecture

GetiDone operates on a three-layer system:

Three-Layer Architecture

  1. Merit & Trust Layer – Determines ranking and credibility
  2. Visibility Layer – Controls exposure via subscription
  3. Priority Experience Layer – Enhances workflow for premium users (both clients and freelancers)

Ranking, visibility, and priority experience are intentionally separated to preserve fairness while rewarding commitment.

Merit-Based Ranking System (Done Score™)

The Done Score™ is GetiDone's proprietary, AI-driven performance metric used to rank professionals across the platform.

Core Ranking Inputs (AI-Weighted)

  • Customer/Client Reviews (sentiment, depth, and consistency)
  • Verified job completion rate
  • Project complexity vs delivery quality
  • Delivery timeliness
  • Communication responsiveness
  • Professional conduct & dispute history
  • Identity, compliance, and trust score (KYC-based)
Important Rule Done Score™ cannot be influenced by payment, subscription, or commission level.
